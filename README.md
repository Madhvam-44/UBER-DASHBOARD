# Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard

  Overview

The Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard delivers a structured and data-driven view of ride operations, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, evaluate trends, and make informed business decisions. The dashboard consolidates key metrics related to bookings, revenue, distance traveled, and customer experience into a single, interactive interface.

  - Key Metrics
   
Completed Bookings: 17K
Lost Bookings: 10K
Total Revenue: 9M
Total Distance Covered: 450.79K
Average Distance per Trip: 24.61

  - Booking Performance

A comparative analysis of completed and lost bookings across months, helping identify demand fluctuations, service inefficiencies, and potential revenue loss.

  - Ride Activity Trends

A monthly trend analysis visualized through a line chart, highlighting peak demand periods and seasonal variations in ride activity.

  - Revenue Analysis

A detailed breakdown of monthly revenue performance, enabling evaluation of financial growth patterns and consistency over time.

  - Revenue by Vehicle Type

Analysis of revenue contribution by vehicle category, where Go Sedan emerges as the highest-performing segment with 9.4M in revenue.

  - Location Intelligence
    
Top Pickup Location: Ashram (117 rides)
Top Drop Location: Narsinghpur (126 rides)
This insight helps identify high-demand zones and optimize ride allocation strategies.

  - Customer Experience Metrics
    
Average Customer Rating: 4.41
Average Driver Rating: 4.23
These indicators reflect overall service quality and customer satisfaction levels.


  - Features

Interactive filtering based on vehicle types
Time-based analysis using Month and Quarter views
Dynamic visualizations for KPI monitoring and trend analysis


  - Purpose

The primary objective of this dashboard is to support operational efficiency and strategic decision-making by:

Monitoring key performance indicators in real time
Identifying demand patterns and operational gaps
Enhancing customer satisfaction and service quality
Enabling data-driven optimization of ride distribution and revenue generation
